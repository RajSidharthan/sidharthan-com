Campaign Management Platform

The Challenge

Udemy's marketing team relied on a fragmented mix of homegrown and third-party tools to run revenue-critical promotional campaigns. The mix of tools created a stressful and negative experience among staff, driving attrition and slowing onboarding productivity.

The Approach

The solution prioritized four key areas: enhanced visual design for improved usability; alignment with marketing industry conventions to ease adoption for new hires; consolidated functionality reducing platform-switching friction; and streamlined interfaces that hid rarely-used advanced features from everyday workflows.

Key Outcomes

A unified campaign management platform that improved day-to-day experience for the marketing team, reduced onboarding time for new hires, and lowered attrition associated with tool frustration.
